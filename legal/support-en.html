CinePunks Support

Need help, want to report a problem, or have an idea for improving CinePunks? Contact CinePunks support by email.

Support email

cinepunks.support@gmail.com

Helpful information to include

When possible, include the following details so we can investigate the problem:

  • the installed CinePunks version;
  • your device model and Android or iOS version;
  • a description of the problem and steps to reproduce it;
  • screenshots that do not contain confidential information.

Do not send passwords, access codes, authentication tokens, or other credentials.

Accounts and connected services

For issues with CinePunks or synchronization, use the support email above. Trakt account management and data stored directly by Trakt remain subject to the tools and terms provided by Trakt.

Privacy and legal documents

You can use the same email address for personal data requests. See the Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions for more information.
